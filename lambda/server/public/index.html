FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MarginArc. Can't find an answer? Reach out to our team.

What is MarginArc?
MarginArc is a margin intelligence platform built natively for Salesforce. It helps channel sales teams analyze deals, optimize pricing, and protect margins using AI-powered scoring and competitive intelligence.
Who is MarginArc built for?
MarginArc is designed for channel sales organizations—particularly VARs, distributors, and OEM partners who need to balance competitive pricing with healthy margins across complex multi-vendor deals.
How does MarginArc improve deal margins?
MarginArc analyzes each deal across multiple signals including deal registration status, competitive landscape, customer relationship strength, and historical win patterns to recommend margin targets that maximize both win probability and profitability.
What Salesforce editions does it support?
MarginArc works with Enterprise, Unlimited, and Performance editions of Salesforce. It is distributed as an AppExchange-ready package.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running in under an hour. MarginArc includes a guided setup wizard that walks administrators through configuration, permission assignment, and optional demo data loading.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. MarginArc processes all data within your Salesforce org and our SOC 2-compliant cloud infrastructure. We use differential privacy techniques and never share customer data across organizations. See our Security documentation for details.
What does the Deal Score mean?
The Deal Score is a composite metric (0–100) that reflects the overall health and margin quality of a deal. It factors in planned margin, competitive pressure, deal registration status, solution complexity, and customer relationship strength.
How does the What-If Simulator work?
The simulator lets you adjust deal parameters—margin percentage, deal registration type, competitive positioning—and instantly see how changes affect your win probability and recommended strategy. It is available directly on each Opportunity record.
Can I see competitive intelligence?
Yes. MarginArc tracks competitors across your pipeline, providing win/loss rates, margin impact analysis, and positioning recommendations. The Competitive Intelligence dashboard shows trends and helps you understand how to win against specific competitors.
Does it integrate with my existing Salesforce workflow?
MarginArc is 100% Salesforce-native. It lives inside your Opportunity records, uses standard Salesforce objects, and requires no external tabs or context switching. Your reps see margin intelligence right where they already work.
What's included in the package?
The MarginArc package includes the Margin Advisor (deal scoring), What-If Simulator, Competitive Intelligence module, Manager Dashboard, Admin Configuration, and a guided Setup Wizard. All components install together as a single managed package.
How is MarginArc licensed?
MarginArc is licensed per-org with seat-based tiers. Contact us for pricing details and to discuss which plan fits your team.
Can I run a proof-of-value evaluation?
Yes. We offer guided POV evaluations where we help you set up MarginArc with demo data or your own historical deals. This lets you see real margin insights before committing. Contact us to get started.
What kind of ROI can I expect?
Customers typically see 2–5 percentage point margin improvements within the first quarter. For a team doing $50M in annual revenue, even a 2-point improvement represents $1M in additional gross profit.
How do I get started?
Visit our Documentation portal to explore the product in detail, or contact us directly to schedule a demo and discuss your team's needs.
